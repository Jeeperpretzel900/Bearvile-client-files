  • Back 2 School

    August 15, 2012

    Hi guys,

    Since I only have a few days left until school starts, I’m playing on Bearville™ as much as I can (plus outside, of course!). There are some really cool new games on Bearville.com™, but I’m super excited about the Carnival in Bearville! You can take a ride on the Ferris Wheel and even meet the Friends Count Bears. It’s all happening on Sandy Bear Island, so be sure to stop by.

    The Friends Count Bears are so cool because they’re pawsome back to school pals and they are also around to help celebrate the Build-A-Bear Workshop 15th birthday this year! (Plus, they have the coolest kicks I’ve ever seen. I seriously wish I had some of those sparkly  fuchsia boots that Friends Count Heart Bear wears.)

    There are three Friends Count Bears: Friends Count Heart Bear, Friends Count Star Bear and Colorful Stars Bear. Even though they are all furbulously fashionable, and it’s really hard to pick just one to bring home.   I’d have to go with Colorful Stars Bear. I mean, come on, have you seen that smile? She is adorable!

    Eeeee! Monday is my first day back to school!

    I’ll fill you in later!

    ChloeRocks™

  • Closing ceremony

    August 13, 2012

    What’s up, guys?

    Wow! Did you see the closing ceremony of the Olympics last night? It was amazing! I loved how London made it like a rock concert and had all kinds of bands there. I was dancing all over the place!

    I am pretty sad that the Olympics are over though. I love being able to watch all  of the different sports each night. Some of the sports I got to watch were archery, track and field events, swimming, gymnastics, and I even got to see different kinds of cycling, like track. Even though it was 17 days long it just seems like it went by so fast!

    I can’t believe there were more than 10,000 athletes from around the world that came to participate! I heard about how much food the swimmers have to eat because they work so hard. Can you imagine trying to feed all those hungry athletes? That’s a ton of food!  And I thought school lunch was a big crowd!

    Speaking of, my very first day of school is next week so I’ve got a lot of planning to do. I’ll tell you about it on Wednesday.

    Peace out!

    ChloeRocks™

  • Take Your Teddy to Work Day – Oct. 11

    August 08, 2012

    What’s up, everyone?

    Did you know there is a Take Your Teddy To Work Day? Even though it's not until October, I still like to plan for it. I’ll be taking my teddy to school. I just have to figure out which one to take because I love them all! Hey, maybe I’ll take one of my smallfrys because I can fit it in my backpack. I’m definitely picking out a furry friend for both Mom and Dad to take to work, too. Hopefully they’ll even take it to meetings and stuff. I love it when the teddy does everything with them. Last year, they both took a ton of pictures and showed them to me at dinner. Since we usually talk about our day at dinner, it was super fun to find out what my furry friends did with them all day. Teddy bears work hard!

    Talk to ya later!
