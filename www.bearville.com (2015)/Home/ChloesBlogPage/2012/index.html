    Hey all you furbulous fans!

    Today is Leap Day. That is SO cool to say. Yup, it’s Leap Day! This day only happens once every four years so it’s pretty cool that I get to talk to you guys on this rare day in history!   • Marvelous Monday

    February 27, 2012

    Hi everyone,

    Today was another great day at school. I love Monday’s because they are a great way to start fresh. Even when it’s rainy, it’s okay, because the day can only get better.

    I also love Monday’s because of my class’s mapping Monday’s that my teacher does each week. This week she asked us to draw Brazil. Have you ever seen Brazil? It is the largest country on the continent of South America. It’s famous for its amazing futbol (pronounced FOOT-ball)  – or soccer team, that almost always makes it to the World Cup, which is played every four years. They’re so good! Futbol is Brazil’s national sport, too, so kids start playing there almost before they can walk!

    You would probably be really good too if you’d been playing soccer that long.

    Peace out!

    ChloeRocks™
