  • Spaghetti squash!

    October 24, 2011

    Hey everybody,

    Squash is a funny thing. Some people totally love it, but some people scrunch up their nose when you talk about it. I used to be a nose-scruncher until I learned how yummy it is and how many different kinds there are. Squash is usually a fall vegetable, even though there are some kinds that you can get early in the year, like zucchini and yellow squash. You’ve probably  tried those and didn’t even realize that they were in the squash family!

    My favorite is spaghetti squash. It’s yellow and oval, and you cut it in half and bake it. When you take it out, you scrape out the insides with a fork and it comes out looking just like spaghetti! I like to eat it with butter and parmesan cheese, but my parents eat it with red sauce like they’re eating real noodles! Ask your parents to try it sometime. It’s a really great afternoon snack, too!

    Have fun!

    ChloeRocks™
