  • Sweet and Sporty!

    March 16, 2011

    I just love holidays! They’re the perfect excuse to get dressed up! And tomorrow,  in honor of St. Patrick’s Day, I already have my whole outfit picked out. Guess what color it’ll be? Green of course! Are you dressing up? I’ve heard a lot of kids have parties at their schools. We are all supposed to bring green snacks to our party, but I haven’t decided what I want to bring yet. 

    I told you how much I love to cook, right! Well, yesterday I made some REALLY great sweet potatoes.  It was super easy. All I did was poke holes in them with a fork a stick them in the microwave for about seven minutes and voilà! Delicious baked sweet potatoes! I will definitely be making those again. Plus, not only do they taste great but they are also good for you.

    Did you know sweet potatoes have vitamins in them that help keep you from getting sick? That makes me like them even more!

    Wanna know something else I love to do? Sports! I especially love to play tennis at the Sportsplex. It’s a great way to get some exercise and hang out with my friends at the same time! Do you play any sports?

    Have an awesome day!

    ChloeRocks™
