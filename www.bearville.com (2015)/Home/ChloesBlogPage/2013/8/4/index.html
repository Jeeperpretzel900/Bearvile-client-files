  • Cody Simpson Koala

    June 26, 2013

    Hello there, readers,

    I am so excited! I heard that Cody Simpson Koala is coming out in Build-A-Bear Workshop stores on Friday and I totally can’t wait. Don’t you think it’s a totally cute koala? Plus, you should see the fashions that it comes with, they are so Cody Simpson’s style. I think it rocks!

    That’s not the only cool thing coming up. Canada Day is on Monday, July 1! It’s just like celebrating Canada’s birthday, so there are lots of celebrations and a lot of people have the day off to take part in them. There are fairs and carnivals and parades and fireworks, and friends and family usually get together at some point to have barbeques and picnics outside. If I were celebrating it, I would definitely have a huge party and invite all my neighbors and friends. What could be a better way to celebrate your country’s birthday than having fun with the people you love in the country you love? Right?

    See ya next time!

  • Growing season

    June 12, 2013

    What’s up?

    Father’s Day is Sunday, June 16. Are you going to celebrate with your family? We usually spend some extra time with dad in the garden or make him a pawsome dinner or something like that.

    My dad has made a totally amazing garden this year. And when I say totally amazing, I mean, usually, he puts in neat rows of lettuce, tomatoes, broccoli, and on and on. But this year he organized his garden into really pretty little sections separated by wood chips and mulch and he is even making a big cage for his tomatoes. For some reason, rabbits, squirrels and birds all love tomatoes. I mean, I guess I can understand because they are pretty delicious, but it’s kind of annoying when you work really hard to grow something so delicious and healthy then find little bites have been taken out of all of them so you can’t even eat them. So, that’s why he made a big cage, because it makes it easier to protect the tomatoes and we can still get to them pretty easily to pick them. I am super impressed.

    The best part is, I get to be his harvest helper, so I get to help pick everything and prepare it for eating. Yum!

    Talk to ya later!

  • Totally ME Tees & Tanks

    June 05, 2013

    Hi, people!

    This Saturday is Best Friend’s Day! So, of course, I’m totally going to hang out with my pals. And I am going to plan for rain, just in case. It’s been really rainy lately and even though I’d really rather play outside I wanted to make sure I’m prepared to play inside, too.

    I am going to bring bleach pens, cardboard and newspaper to my friend’s house on Saturday when we hang out so we can make bleach pen T-shirts and tanks. We could even decorate jean shorts or something; that would be really cute! Here is a picture of one I did with my mom last year. It’s really easy. Just make sure you protect your work area so you don’t accidentally bleach a couch or chair or even worse, something fab from your closet that you totally love! Eeek! Anyway, once you follow the directions on the back of the bleach pen for how to make it work, just draw whatever design you want on your old or reused or plain piece of clothing that you’ve decided to decorate. I like tank tops in the summer because it gets really hot where I live, (but I always make sure to wear sunscreen when I know I’m going to be outside.)

    Have sun!
