    What’s up friends?

    So I don’t know if you know this, but most of our friends in the United Kingdom are just now coming up on what is called Half-Term. That means that they have a break from school. But most of the people I’ve been fishing with are awesome. Some people think you absolutely have to go fishing in the morning, but I also like fishing in the afternoon. It’s so relaxing.     Hey guys!

    I can’t wait! Smurfs come out Friday at Build-A-Bear Workshop stores in the United Kingdom, and they are already out in the United States and Canada. And the new movie The Smurfs 2 comes out later this month. I love Smurfette, cause she just has fab style all the time. But there are two others that I want, Vexy and Hackus, and both are adorable! It will be very hard to decide, cause I want them all!

    You know how Smurfette wears an adorable white dress? Well, this summer I’ve totally been loving white. I’ve saved my allowance every week to buy something white for my wardrobe, cause I think white is just totally summery and fresh looking! Plus, it’s kind of like black because it goes with everything, you know? I have a white flowy skirt that I wear all the time and I also have a pair of white sandals that I’ll probably wear out by the end of the summer because I wear those with everything.  They even make jeans and a T-shirt look cute!

  • Independence Day!

    July 04, 2013

    Hi friends,

    Tomorrow is a beary big day for us in the United States, it’s Independence Day, or as some people like to call it, the Fourth of July! Most people will celebrate by watching parades with friends and family, going to fairs or carnivals, barbequing, maybe going to the pool and then watching fireworks at night. I always watch our neighborhood parade with my family, and a lot of the people who live on the road that the parade goes down have parties at their house afterwards or they put up chairs and tables all over their yards like a theater.  It’s kind of a big deal.

    I think my favorite part of the parade is when the high school marching band comes by. I can’t wait to be in the marching band. I love hearing the marching drums from really far away, they’re so loud! I play piano right now, but obviously I can’t play the piano and march at the same time. So I will hopefully be learning flute, or maybe even piccolo! The piccolo is that tiny little flute-looking instrument. You hold it the same way as a flute, sideways, and my dad always says the piccolo gets the coolest part of the real marching songs. Plus, I feel like it probably wouldn’t be that much work to hold up and march with because it’s so tiny. So yeah, I think I’ll claim piccolo, when the time comes.

    Happy 4th of July!

    ChloeRocks™
