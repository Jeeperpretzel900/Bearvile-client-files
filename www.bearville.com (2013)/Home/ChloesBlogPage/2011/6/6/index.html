  • Easter!

    April 20, 2011

    Hey everyone!

    Yay! Easter is this weekend! I hope it’s sunny because I got a beautiful new yellow dress and I can’t wait to wear it. I have some cute white sandals that would match perfectly, but I also found some Mary Jane’s in my closet that I’d totally forgotten about, so, maybe I’ll wear those instead? What are you wearing this Easter?

    I also can’t wait for the Easter Egg Hunt! My family always has a really big one and I’m the best at finding the eggs. After the hunt my family goes inside to have a brunch and we play an egg cracking game. It’s kind of a tradition. Two people pick the egg of their choice then hold it in their hand and gently start tapping the other person’s egg with their own. The point is to try to crack the other person’s egg first. My dad rocks at this game! What are your Easter traditions?

    Again, you only have a few days left to enter our awesome sweepstakes. You could win a trip to NYC and a chance to host one of our shows on Bearville!

    Happy Easter!
