  • What’s coming?

    April 13, 2011

    Hi friends!

    There are so many cool things coming up this month that it’s hard to keep track! So, I’ll give you a preview of all the fun to come.

    First of all, Build-A-Bear Workshop® has a new furry friend – Greenzys (you pronounce it green-z’s) Panda! He is SO CUTE and earth friendly with fur made of soy! Visit Greenzys Panda at the Pawsitively Green® Recycling Center to to get some cool ideas on how to help the environment! And since Earth Day is just around the corner (April 20) it might be good to at least start thinking about how you can help the planet.

    And before I go, I just wanted to remind you not to forget to enter our amazing sweeps if you haven’t already. You only have a little more than a week left. Believe me, you do not want to miss this chance to host one of our shows on Bearville.com™. Oh yeah, and the winner gets a trip to NYC, so it’s pretty sweet if I do say so myself.

    Good luck!

    ChloeRocks™
